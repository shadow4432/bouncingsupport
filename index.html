Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my game lagging?
Try lowering graphics settings, closing background apps, or restarting your headset/device.
How do I update Bouncing VR?
Download the newest version from the official game page or launcher.
Multiplayer is not working.
Check your internet connection and make sure both players are on the latest version.
Can I report cheaters or exploiters?
Yes. Include screenshots or video proof when contacting support.
